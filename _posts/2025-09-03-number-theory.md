This post demonstrates how to use **MathJax** with Jekyll Minima.

### Inline math
You can write inline math like this: $E = mc^2$ or equivalently \(\frac{a}{b}\).

### Display math
And block equations like this:

$$
\int_{0}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} \, dx = \frac{\sqrt{\pi}}{2}
$$

Or using `\[ ... \]` syntax:

$$
\nabla \cdot \vec{E} = \frac{\rho}{\epsilon_0}
$$
